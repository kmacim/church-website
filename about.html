About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the love of God, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with compassion and dedication. We believe in creating a welcoming environment where everyone can find hope, healing, and purpose.

Our Values

Love

We are committed to showing unconditional love to God and to one another, reflecting Christ’s example.

Community

Our Core Beliefs

  • We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in the divine inspiration and authority of the Holy Bible.
  • We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His atoning death, His bodily resurrection, and His ascension.
  • We believe in the salvation of lost and sinful humanity through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost.
